21-WEEK MARATHON TRAINING PLAN

Start Date: February 9, 2026

Race Week: June 29-July 5, 2026

Race Day (Convergence): 04 July 2026

Goal: Build aerobic capacity, strength, and speed for a successful marathon.

CORE TRAINING PRINCIPLES

  • ► 4 Runs Per Week: Monday, Wednesday, Thursday (recovery), Saturday (long run)
  • ► Weekday Cap: Maximum 10 km or 60 minutes on Mon/Wed/Thu
  • ► MAF HR Discipline: MAF threshold at 148 bpm, Zone 2: 132-147 bpm
  • ► Strength Training: Tuesday & Friday using ATG (Knees Over Toes) methodology
  • ► Mileage Progression: +10% weekly, deload every 4th week (-20%)
  • ► Long Run Peak: Build to 32-34 km before taper
  • ► Speed Work: Introduced Week 14+ (intervals, strides, tempo runs)
  • ► Taper: Weeks 20-21 reduce volume by 40-60%
  • ► Recovery: Sunday is rest & mobility work only
