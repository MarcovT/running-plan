CORE TRAINING RULES

  • 4 Runs per week: Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday.
  • Weekday cap: max 8 km or 60 min on Mon/Wed/Thu.
  • Heart-rate discipline: Easy 132-148 bpm (cap 150), marathon-effort work mostly 146-154 bpm.
  • Strength: Tuesday and Friday ATG sessions, lighter in Weeks 1-2 and taper weeks.
  • Long-run priority: Protect Saturday. If busy/fatigued, cut Wednesday quality first.
  • Illness guardrail: If HR is >5 bpm high at normal easy pace for 2 runs, reduce week volume by ~20%.
